# <a name="view-posted-sales-tax-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="65714-103">Visualizza transazioni IVA registrate</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-103">View posted sales tax transactions</span></span>

[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="65714-104">È possibile visualizzare le transazioni IVA registrate da diverse pagine a seconda di cosa è necessario visualizzare.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-104">You can view posted sales tax transactions from various page depending on what you need to view.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="65714-105">In questa attività viene utilizzata la società dimostrativa USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-105">This task uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="65714-106">Passare a Imposta > Richieste di informazioni e report > Richieste di informazioni su IVA > IVA registrata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-106">Go to Tax > Inquiries and reports > Sales tax inquiries > Posted sales tax.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="65714-107">Fare clic su Mostra filtri.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-107">Click Show filters.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="65714-108">Chiudere la pagina.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-108">Close the page.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="65714-109">Andare a Imposta > Imposte indirette > IVA > Periodi liquidazione IVA.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-109">Go to Tax > Indirect taxes > Sales tax > Sales tax settlement periods.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="65714-110">Espandere la sezione Intervalli periodici.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-110">Expand the Period intervals section.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="65714-111">Selezionare l'intervallo a cui si è interessati.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-111">Select the interval you are interested in.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="65714-112">Visualizzare le transazioni IVA registrate relative all'intervallo periodico della liquidazione selezionata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-112">Display posted sales tax transactions for the selected settlement period interval.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="65714-113">È possibile filtrare ulteriormente l'elenco delle transazioni IVA registrate.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-113">You can further filter the list of posted sales tax transactions.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="65714-114">Chiudere la pagina.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-114">Close the page.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="65714-115">Andare a Imposta > Imposte indirette > IVA > Codici IVA.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-115">Go to Tax > Indirect taxes > Sales tax > Sales tax codes.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="65714-116">Nel riquadro azioni, fare clic su Codice IVA.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-116">On the Action Pane, click Sales tax code.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="65714-117">Fare clic su IVA registrata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="65714-117">Click Posted sales tax.</span></span>
