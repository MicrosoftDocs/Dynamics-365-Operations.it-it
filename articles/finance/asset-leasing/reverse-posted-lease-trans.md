<span data-ttu-id="f3be2-105">Qualsiasi transazione creata tramite Leasing cespite può essere stornata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3be2-105">Any transaction that is created through Asset leasing can be reversed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3be2-106">Le transazioni stornate tramite Leasing cespite aggiornano i dati del leasing.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3be2-106">Transactions that are reversed through Asset leasing update your lease data.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3be2-107">Pertanto, aggiornano anche i valori contabili dell'obbligazione sul leasing e l'Asset Right of use (ROU).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3be2-107">Therefore, they also update the carrying values of the lease liability and right-of-use (ROU) asset.</span></span>
