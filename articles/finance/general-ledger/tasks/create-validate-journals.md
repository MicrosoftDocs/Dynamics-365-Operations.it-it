[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="7ed74-104">Questa procedura crea e convalida giornali di registrazione e righe del giornale di registrazione.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-104">This procedure creates and validates journals and journal lines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-105">Puoi provare questa procedura utilizzando la società di dati dimostrativi USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-105">You can try this procedure using the USMF demo company.</span></span>  

1. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-106">Fare clic su **Contabilità generale > Scritture contabili > Giornali di registrazione generali**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-106">Go to **General ledger > Journal entries > General journals**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-107">Fare clic su **Nuovo**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-107">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-108">Nel campo **Nome** fare clic sul pulsante a discesa per aprire la ricerca.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-108">In the **Name** field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-109">Trovare e selezionare il record desiderato nell'elenco.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-109">In the list, find and select the desired record.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-110">Fare clic su **Righe**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-110">Click **Lines**.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-111">Nel campo **Conto** immetti un conto appropriato in base al tipo di conto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-111">In the **Account** field enter an appropriate account based on the Account type.</span></span>

8. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-113">Immetti un importo per il conto in **Dare** o in **Avere**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-113">Enter an amount for the account as either a **Debit** or **Credit**.</span></span>

10. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-115">Fare clic su **Convalida**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-115">Click **Validate**.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-116">Fare clic su **Convalida**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-116">Click **Validate**.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-117">Fare clic su **Registra**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-117">Click **Post**.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="7ed74-118">Fai clic su **Giustificativo**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ed74-118">Click **Voucher**.</span></span>
