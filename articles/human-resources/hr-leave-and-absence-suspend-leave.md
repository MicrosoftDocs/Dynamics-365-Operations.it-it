## <a name="suspend-leave-and-absence-for-an-employee"></a><span data-ttu-id="1b34b-105">Sospendere congedi e assenze per un dipendente</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1b34b-105">Suspend leave and absence for an employee</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="1b34b-106">Nel record del dipendente, selezionare **Congedo**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1b34b-106">On the employee's record, select **Leave**.</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="1b34b-107">Selezionare **Sospendi congedo**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1b34b-107">Select **Suspend leave**.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="1b34b-108">Selezionare **Nuovo**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1b34b-108">Select **New**.</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="1b34b-109">Nella finestra di dialogo **Sospendi accumuli di congedi**, selezionare **Tipo di congedo** con la **Data d'inizio** e la **Data di fine** della sospensione.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1b34b-109">In the **Suspend leave accrual** dialog box, select the **Leave type** along with the **Start date** and **End date** for the suspension.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="1b34b-111">Se gli accumuli vengono elaborati mentre il congedo del dipendente è sospeso, non verranno effettuati accumuli per i tipi di congedo sospesi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1b34b-111">If accruals are processed while the employee's leave is suspended, no accrual will be made for the suspended leave types.</span></span>
