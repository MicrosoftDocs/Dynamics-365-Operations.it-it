[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="f6d6b-104">Fare clic su **Gestione assistenza** \> **Impostazione** \> **Ordini di assistenza** \> **Fasi di assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d6b-104">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service orders** \> **Service stages**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="f6d6b-105">Premere CTRL+N per creare un nuovo record.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d6b-105">Press CTRL+N to create a new record.</span></span>
