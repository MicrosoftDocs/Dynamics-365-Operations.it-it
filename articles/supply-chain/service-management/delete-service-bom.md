# <a name="delete-a-service-bom"></a><span data-ttu-id="f1260-103">Eliminare una DBA assistenza</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1260-103">Delete a service BOM</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="f1260-104">Fare clic su **Gestione assistenza** \> **Comune** \> **Contratti di assistenza** \> **Contratti di assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1260-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f1260-105">Selezionare il contratto di assistenza da cui si desidera eliminare una distinta base (DBA) assistenza.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1260-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="f1260-106">Nel **riquadro azioni**, fare clic su **Oggetti assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1260-106">On the **Action Pane**, click **Service objects**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="f1260-107">Nel modulo **Oggetti assistenza** selezionare l'oggetto assistenza da cui si desidera eliminare una DBA assistenza.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1260-107">In the **Service objects** form, select the service object that you want to delete a service BOM from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f1260-108">Fare clic su **Funzioni** quindi selezionare **Elimina DBA assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1260-108">Click **Functions**, and then select **Delete service BOM**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="f1260-109">Fare clic su **Sì**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1260-109">Click **Yes**.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="f1260-110">Se non viene applicata una DBA modello, è possibile eliminare la DBA assistenza dal modulo <STRONG>DBA modello</STRONG>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1260-110">If you have not applied a template BOM, the service BOM can be deleted from the <STRONG>Template BOMs</STRONG> form.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f1260-111">Se è stata applicata una DBA modello, la DBA assistenza non può essere eliminata utilizzando questo metodo.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1260-111">If you have applied a template BOM, the service BOM cannot be deleted by using this method.</span></span></P>
