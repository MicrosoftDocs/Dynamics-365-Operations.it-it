[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="52cbc-104">Utilizzare la procedura seguente per impostare un modello per la rappresentazione di oggetti nella sede del cliente.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="52cbc-104">Use this procedure to set up a template that represents objects at a customer site.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="52cbc-105">Fare clic su **Gestione assistenza** \> **Comune** \> **Contratti di assistenza** \> **Contratti di assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="52cbc-105">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="52cbc-106">Fare doppio clic su un contratto di assistenza oppure fare clic su **Contratto di assistenza** per crearne uno nuovo.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="52cbc-106">Double-click a service agreement, or click **Service agreement** to create a new service agreement.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="52cbc-107">Fare clic sulla scheda **Impostazioni**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="52cbc-107">Click the **Setup** tab.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="52cbc-108">Per collegare una distinta base (DBA) modello al contratto di assistenza fare clic su **Oggetti assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="52cbc-108">Click **Service objects** to attach a template bill of materials (BOM) to the service agreement.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="52cbc-109">Nel modulo **Oggetti assistenza**, selezionare l'oggetto assistenza, quindi fare clic su **Funzioni** \> **Collega DBA modello**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="52cbc-109">In the **Service objects** form, select a service object, and then click **Functions** \> **Attach template BOM**.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="52cbc-110">Nel modulo **Seleziona DBA modello**, selezionare una DBA modello nel campo **DBA modello**, quindi fare clic su **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="52cbc-110">In the **Select template BOM** form, select a template BOM in the **Template BOM** field, and then click **OK**.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="52cbc-111">Nel modello **Oggetti asistenza**, fare clic su **Designer** per modificare la DBA modello in modo da rappresentare gli oggetti desiderati.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="52cbc-111">In the **Service objects** form, click **Designer** to modify the template BOM so that the BOM represents the objects that you want.</span></span>

8.  <span data-ttu-id="52cbc-112">Chiudere i moduli per salvare le modifiche.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="52cbc-112">Close the forms to save your changes.</span></span>
