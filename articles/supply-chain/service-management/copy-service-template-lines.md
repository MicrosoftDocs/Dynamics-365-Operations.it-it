# <a name="copy-service-templates-lines"></a><span data-ttu-id="477ee-103">Copiare le righe di modelli di assistenza</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-103">Copy service templates lines</span></span> 

[!include[banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="477ee-104">È possibile copiare le informazioni di un modello in un contratto di assistenza o in un ordine di assistenza.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-104">Template information can be copied to either a service agreement or a service order.</span></span>

## <a name="copy-service-template-lines-into-a-service-agreement"></a><span data-ttu-id="477ee-105">Copiare righe modello di assistenza in un contratto di assistenza</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-105">Copy service template lines into a service agreement</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="477ee-106">Fare clic su **Contratti di assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-106">Click **Service agreements**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="477ee-107">Fare doppio clic su un contratto di assistenza per aprire la pagina **Contratti di assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-107">Double-click a service agreement to open the **Service agreements** page.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="477ee-108">Fare clic su **Righe modello** per aprire la pagina **Copia righe modello di assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-108">Click **Template lines** to open the **Copy service template lines** page.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="477ee-109">Nella scheda **Controllo struttura** espandere la struttura, individuare il modello contenente le righe che si desidera copiare nel contratto di assistenza e selezionarlo.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-109">On the **Tree control** tab, expand the tree, find the template that contains the lines that you want to copy to the service agreement, and then select the service template.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="477ee-111">Fare clic su **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-111">Click **OK**.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="477ee-112">Chiudere le pagine per salvare le modifiche.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-112">Close the pages to save your changes.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="477ee-113">Le righe del modello di assistenza verranno copiate nel contratto di assistenza con le relazioni oggetti assistenza e attività di assistenza collegate.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="477ee-113">The service template lines are now copied to the service agreement, together with the attached service object and service task relations.</span></span>
