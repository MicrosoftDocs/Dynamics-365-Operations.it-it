# <a name="attach-templates-to-service-objects"></a><span data-ttu-id="069c9-103">Collegare modelli a oggetti assistenza</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="069c9-103">Attach templates to service objects</span></span>    

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="069c9-104">Una distinta base (DBA) modello può essere collegata a una relazione tra oggetti assistenza.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="069c9-104">A template bill of materials (BOM) can be attached to a service object relation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="069c9-105">Quest'ultima potrà quindi essere associata a ordini di assistenza o a contratti di assistenza.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="069c9-105">The service object relation can then be associated with either service orders or service agreements.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="069c9-106">Fare clic su **Gestione assistenza** \> **Impostazione** \> **Oggetti assistenza** \> **Oggetti assistenza**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="069c9-106">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service objects** \> **Service objects**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="069c9-107">Selezionare un oggetto assistenza.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="069c9-107">Select a service object.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="069c9-108">Fare clic su **Contratti di assistenza** o su **Ordini di assistenza** per visualizzare i contratti di assistenza o gli ordini di assistenza a cui è stato associato l'oggetto assistenza.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="069c9-108">Click **Service agreements** or **Service orders** to display the service agreements or service orders that the service object has been associated with.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="069c9-109">Selezionare un contratto o un ordine di assistenza, fare clic su **Funzioni** e quindi su **Collega DBA modello**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="069c9-109">Select a service agreement or a service order, click **Functions**, and then click **Attach template BOM**.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="069c9-110">Selezionare la DBA modello da collegare all'oggetto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="069c9-110">Select the template BOM to attach to the object.</span></span>


> [!TIP]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="069c9-111">Per eliminare una DBA collegata o spostarla in un altro oggetto assistenza, fare clic su <STRONG>Funzioni</STRONG> nel modulo <STRONG>Oggetti assistenza</STRONG>, quindi scegliere <STRONG>Sposta DBA assistenza</STRONG> o <STRONG>Elimina DBA assistenza</STRONG>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="069c9-111">To move an attached BOM to another service object or to delete an attached BOM, in the <STRONG>Service objects</STRONG> form, click <STRONG>Functions</STRONG>, and then click <STRONG>Move service BOM</STRONG> or <STRONG>Delete service BOM</STRONG>.</span></span></P>
