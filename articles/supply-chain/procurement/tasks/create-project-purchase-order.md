2. <span data-ttu-id="51f55-107">Nell'elenco fare clic sul collegamento nella riga selezionata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51f55-107">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="51f55-115">Fare clic su OK.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51f55-115">Click OK.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="51f55-116">Nell'elenco contrassegnare la riga selezionata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51f55-116">In the list, mark the selected row.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="51f55-117">Nel campo Numero di articoli immettere o selezionare un valore.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51f55-117">In the Item number field, enter or select a value.</span></span>

13. <span data-ttu-id="51f55-120">Fare clic sulla scheda Progetto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51f55-120">Click the Project tab.</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="51f55-121">Verificare che i prezzi di costo e di vendita siano disponibili.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51f55-121">Verify that the sales and cost prices are available.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="51f55-122">Se non sono disponibili ma sono necessari, immettere le informazioni.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51f55-122">If they are not available but needed, enter the information.</span></span>
