<span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-104">Fondamentalmente, la pianificazione generale consente alle società di determinare ed equilibrare il fabbisogno futuro di materie prime e la capacità di soddisfare gli obiettivi aziendali.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-104">At its core, Master planning allows companies to determine and balance the future need for raw materials and capacity to meet company goals.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-105">La pianificazione generale valuta quanto segue:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-105">Master planning assesses the following:</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-106">Quali sono le materie prime e le capacità attualmente disponibili?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-106">What raw materials and capacities are currently available?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-110">I tre principali processi di progettazione sono:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-110">The three main planning processes are:</span></span>

-  <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-111">**Pianificazione generale** - Il piano generale calcola i fabbisogni netti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-111">**Master planning** - The Master plan calculates net requirements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-112">Si basa sugli ordini correnti effettivi e consente alle società di controllare il rifornimento delle scorte a breve termine, su base quotidiana.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-112">It is based on actual current orders and enables companies to control inventory replenishment on a short-term, day-to-day basis.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-113">Un altro termine per descriverlo è *Piano dei fabbisogni netti*.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-113">Another term to describe it is the *Net requirements plan*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-114">Per ulteriori informazioni, vedere [Pianificazione generale](master-plans.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-114">For more information, see [Master planning](master-plans.md).</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-115">**Pianificazione previsionale** - La programmazione delle previsioni calcola i fabbisogni lordi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-115">**Forecast planning** - The Forecast schedule calculates gross requirements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-116">Si basa su proiezioni future (o previsioni) e consente alle società di condurre pianificazioni di materie prime e di capacità a lungo termine.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-116">It is based on future projections (or forecasts), and enables companies to conduct long-term planning of materials and capacity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-117">Per ulteriori informazioni, vedere [Pianificazione previsionale](introduction-demand-forecasting.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-117">For more information, see [Forecast planning](introduction-demand-forecasting.md).</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-118">**Pianificazione generale interaziendale** - Il piano generale interaziendale calcola i fabbisogni netti tra le persone giuridiche.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-118">**Intercompany master planning** - The Intercompany master plan calculates net requirements across legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-119">Collega la domanda e l'offerta tra società non solo per la domanda e l'offerta a breve termine e costanti ma anche per la domanda e l'offerta pianificate (non ancora consolidate) a lungo termine.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-119">It connects demand and supply between companies not only for short term, firm demand and supply but also for long-term, planned (that is not yet firmed) demand and supply.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-120">Per ulteriori informazioni, vedere [Pianificazione generale interaziendale](https://mbspartner.microsoft.com/AX/CourseOverview/1276) (eLearning) (richiede l'account CustomerSource).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-120">For more information, see [Intercompany master planning](https://mbspartner.microsoft.com/AX/CourseOverview/1276)  (eLearning) (requires CustomerSource account).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-121">Le società possono modificare l'output del piano.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-121">Companies can change the output of the plan.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-122">Possono eseguire la modifica netta o rigenerativa o entrambi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-122">They can run regenerative, net change, or both.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-123">I piani rigeneratori aggiornano tutti i fabbisogni, mentre, i piani di modifica netta aggiornano solo il piano degli articoli con nuovi requisiti che sono stati inseriti dopo l'esecuzione dell'ultima programmazione.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-123">Regenerative plans update all requirements, whereas, net change plans only update the plan on items with new requirements that have come in since the last scheduling run.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-124">I piani di programmazione generale sono in genere a breve termine, quindi nell'ordine da una settimana a sei mesi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-124">Master scheduling plans typically involve the short term, which can be anywhere from one week to six months.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-125">Il modulo di pianificazione generale determina le esigenze di fornitura (materiali) e di capacità (risorse) che risponderanno alla domanda corrente (fabbisogni netti).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-125">The Master planning module determines the supply (materials) and capacity (resources) needs that will meet current demand (the net requirements).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ee4bc-126">Nella maggior parte delle società questo viene esteso per includere il lead time cumulativo massimo tra i prodotti da ricevere.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ee4bc-126">In most companies this is extended to include the longest cumulative lead time among the products to be received.</span></span>
