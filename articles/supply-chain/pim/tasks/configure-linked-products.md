<span data-ttu-id="3e56f-104">In questa procedura vengono descritti i passaggi per configurare i prodotti collegati.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-104">This procedure walks through configuring linked products.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-105">Questa procedura per i dati dimostrativi USRT e presuppone che entrambi i prodotti siano già stata creati e rilasciati.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-105">This procedure uses the USRT demo data, and assumes both products have already been created and released.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-106">Fare clic su Gestione informazioni sul prodotto > Prodotti > Prodotti rilasciati.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-106">Go to Product information management > Products > Released products.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-107">Nell'elenco trovare e selezionare il record desiderato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-107">In the list, find and select the desired record.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-108">Nel riquadro azioni fare clic su Retail e Commerce.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-108">On the Action Pane, click Retail and Commerce.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-109">Fare clic su Prodotti collegati.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-109">Click Linked products.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-110">Fare clic su Modifica.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-110">Click Edit.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-111">Nel campo Numero prodotto collegato fare clic sul pulsante a discesa per aprire la ricerca.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-111">In the Linked product number field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-112">Nell'elenco fare clic sul collegamento nella riga selezionata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-112">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-113">Nel campo Unità fare clic sul pulsante a discesa per aprire la ricerca.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-113">In the Unit field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-114">Nell'elenco fare clic sul collegamento nella riga selezionata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-114">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-115">Nel campo Quantità immettere un numero.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-115">In the Quantity field, enter a number.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="3e56f-116">Fare clic su Salva.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e56f-116">Click Save.</span></span>
