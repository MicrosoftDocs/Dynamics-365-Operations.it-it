[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="25958-107">Gli ordini di lavoro possono essere creati nei seguenti modi:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="25958-107">Work orders can be created in the following ways:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="25958-108">Per i piani di manutenzione basati su calendario in cui è attivata l'impostazione "Crea automaticamente", automaticamente tramite [Piani di manutenzione programmata](../preventive-and-reactive-maintenance/schedule-maintenance-plans.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="25958-108">For  calendar-based maintenance plans where the "Auto create" setting is turned on, automatically by using [Schedule maintenance plans](../preventive-and-reactive-maintenance/schedule-maintenance-plans.md).</span></span>
