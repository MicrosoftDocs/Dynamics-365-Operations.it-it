<span data-ttu-id="983ce-107">È anche possibile impostare cicli di manutenzione in unità funzionali.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="983ce-107">You can also set up maintenance rounds class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="983ce-108">Maintenance plans on assets are active regardless of where the asset is installed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="983ce-109">I piani di manutenzione
